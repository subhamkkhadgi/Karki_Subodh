Depression

On this page, you can find articles, videos, and take a survey related to depression to better understand and manage it.

Remedies

1. Engage in physical activity to improve mood.

2. Build a support network of friends and family.

3. Seek therapy or counseling for professional help.

Causes of Depression

1. Traumatic life events.

2. Chemical imbalances in the brain.

3. Family history of depression.
